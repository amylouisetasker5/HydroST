What is Hydrocephalus?

According to the hydrocephalus and Spina Bifida charity shine “Hydrocephalus is a condition in which fluid in the brain that normally flows away, builds up causing pressure and sever pain.“

What is a Shunt.

According to the hydrocephalus and Spina Bifida “A shunt is a device fitted to help this fluid drain away.”
